Good Anime

This site is a listing of anime that is good to watch.

Hunter X Hunter©

A young boy sets off from home to follow in the footsteps of his father hopinmg one day they may meet again. along the way he finds new friends and new skills.

Shiki©

Death takes on new meaning when outsiders relocate to a sleepy mountain town. Cutoff from the outside work town members begin dying at a rapid pace.

RWBY©

The story of four young women fighting evil and finding themselves in the process.

Death Note©

A young man is granted unspeakable power to cause death. A cat and mouse game between one that takes life and one that tries to protect it.

Bleach©

The story of a high school student with an uncanny ability to step into the spiritual world. He and his pals fight tainted souls and build comrades within the netherworld.

Fruits Basket©

Is a tale of love and acceptance. In this tale, people are not as they seem. The pressure to live upto expectations shapes young lives and young love.

Attack on Titam©

Humans are huddled together in a post-apocalyptic world. A war with what seems like an invincible enemy has decimated humanity. Those remaining are driven behind three circular walls. These walled cities are the last thing between linving and destruction.

My Hero Akademia©

In a world full of heroes, one boy longs to join their ranks. However, being born without any special power his dreams may be dashed before he has a chance to show the strength of his heart.

Akagami No Shirayukihime©

A beautiful interpretation of the Snow White story. Both the main characters strong and likeable. This is a romance about two people who met unexpectedly and through friendship fall in love.

Naruto©

Is the story of a young boy growing up in the shadow of war. Alone in his village he seeks acceptance from his fellow villagers. To achieve his goal he becomes a shinobi and uses his uncanny ability to win over others to win the heart and allegiance of villagers and enemies.
